Returns with Bitcoin

The following quiltcharts highlight historical sector-level dividend and stock price growth data (in addition to S&P 500 data). These charts can be used to view market sector trends on an annual basis, even for the current year. The default quilt chart will show the dividend growth trends by sector. To view the stock price return click the right button and a blue border will appear around the button. Hover over a market sector you are interested in learning more about to highlight the historical data for that sector. Cumulative and annualized growth rates are provided on the right.
